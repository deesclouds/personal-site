About

As a creative and driven individual with a passion for art and technology, I have always been interested in finding ways to use my skills and experiences to make a positive impact on the world.


After attending the Fashion Institute of Technology and working as an art assistant for local artists, I pursued my love of photography at Pace University, where I received a degree in photography with minors in non-profit studies and queer studies. My work has been showcased at the International Center of Photography, and I have also had the opportunity to share my knowledge and passion for photography as a teaching assistant for aspiring photographers.


In recent years, I have also been focused on expanding my skills in software engineering, digital marketing and technical writing. I am excited to use these skills to continue creating magical experiences for the broader public through the use of code and technology. I am eager to continue learning and growing as a professional, and I am always looking for new opportunities to use my skills and talents to make a positive difference.


Feel free to follow or reach out via my social links!
